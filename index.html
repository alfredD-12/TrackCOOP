Transforming Cooperative Management

TrackCOOP represents a revolutionary shift from traditional, manual paper-based recordkeeping to a sophisticated digital infrastructure. Our platform eliminates the inefficiencies of physical document handling, replacing them with intelligent automation and secure cloud storage.

Built specifically for the Nasugbu Farmers and Fisherfolks Agriculture Cooperative, TrackCOOP combines cutting-edge OCR technology, predictive analytics, and real-time dashboards to provide unprecedented visibility into cooperative operations.

With role-based access control, automated member engagement tracking, and sector-specific communication tools, TrackCOOP empowers every stakeholder--from the Chairman to individual members--with the tools they need to succeed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TrackCOOP

How do I access my TrackCOOP account?
Click the "Access Portal" or "Log In to Portal" button on this page. You'll need your registered email address and password. If you're a new member, your credentials will be provided by the cooperative administrator during onboarding.
What happens to the physical documents after scanning?
Physical documents are securely archived after being digitized through our OCR scanning process. The digital copies are stored in encrypted cloud storage with automatic backups. Original documents are retained according to cooperative policy and regulatory requirements.
Is my share capital data secure?
Absolutely. TrackCOOP employs bank-level encryption for all financial data. Your share capital information is protected with role-based access control, meaning only authorized users can view your specific data. All transactions are logged and audited for maximum transparency and security.
